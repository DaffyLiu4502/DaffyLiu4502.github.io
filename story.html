REDISTRICTING & SEGREGETION

Audio Story

Listen it on Youtube

Columbia Public School District Board recently approved new school attendance boundaries for secondary schools which was implemented this fall. A new middle school will be added on Columbia’s south side to help address overcrowding at certain schools throughout the area. However, each time the district redraws attendance boundaries, it has the chance to prevent othering; yet, it’s happening both in Columbia and nationally.
